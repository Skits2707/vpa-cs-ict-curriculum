Section 5 — BilingualTeaching Practice

BilingualTeaching Practice

Technical SubjectChinese'sEALScaffolding。EveryEvery Student Should Have AccessComputer ScienceLanguage'sPathway。

inBilingualTeaching CS in this environment is more than translation——but building conceptual bridges。Every technical term pairedEALEvery learner has potential barriers。Me'sMethodSystematically removing these barriers。

Me'sMethod

BilingualKeywords First — Displayed at Start of Every Lesson3-5EnglishChinesecomparison'sKeyTerminology。StudentsinSee Vocabulary Before Encountering Concepts，Preparing for Understanding。

VisualScaffolding — Diagrams、Flowchartsand Physical Demos Reduce Language Dependence。Students Can Understand by Moving Physical CardsBinaryAddition，Without Reading Any Sentences。

Sentence Start — For extended written answers，I provide materials in both languagesSentence Start。This removed"Blank Page"Anxiety，Letting students focus on demonstrating technical understanding。
