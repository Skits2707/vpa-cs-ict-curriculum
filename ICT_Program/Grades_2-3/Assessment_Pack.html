Grades 2–3 Assessment Pack

Program: Digital Foundations | Assessment Types: Formative (ongoing) & Summative (term-end)

Assessment Philosophy

For Grades 2–3, assessment is primarily observational and portfolio-based. Young EAL learners demonstrate understanding through doing, creating, and speaking rather than traditional written tests. All assessments include bilingual support and visual aids.

EAL Support Strategies in Assessment

  • Visual Supports: All assessments use picture cues, icons, and color coding
  • Bilingual Glossaries: Key terms displayed in English, Chinese characters, and Pinyin
  • Sentence Frames: "I like my story because..." / "I learned to..." / "I can..."
  • Peer Translation: Bilingual students may assist with instructions (not answers)
  • Extended Time: Bronze-level students receive additional time for all tasks
  • Retakes: Students may retry practical assessments after additional practice

Parent Communication

Each term, send home a bilingual progress report including:

  • Smiley face self-assessment from student
  • Teacher comments in English + Chinese summary
  • Photo of best work sample
  • 2–3 suggested activities for home practice
