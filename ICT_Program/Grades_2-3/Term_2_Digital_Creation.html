Term 2: Digital Creation — Weeks 13–24

Theme: Digital art, word processing, digital storytelling | Duration: 40 minutes per lesson

Term Overview

Students transition from learning computer basics to creating original digital content. Using age-appropriate art software and word processors, students create digital artwork, simple documents, and multimedia stories. Emphasis on creativity, self-expression, and building English writing confidence through bilingual support.

Week-by-Week Scheme of Work

Week 13 — Introduction to Digital Art

Keywords: digital art 数字艺术 (shùzì yìshù), paint brush 画笔 (huàbǐ), eraser 橡皮擦 (xiàngpí cā), canvas 画布 (huàbù), color palette 调色板 (tiáosè bǎn)
Activity 1 (15 min): "Explore Tux Paint" — Free exploration time. Students click each tool and draw a small picture. Teacher circulates naming tools in English and Chinese.

Activity 2 (15 min): "Tool Matching" — Students match tool icons to their names on a worksheet.

Activity 3 (10 min): "My First Masterpiece" — Students draw their favorite animal using at least 3 different tools.
Formative: Did the student use at least 3 tools intentionally?

Week 14 — Drawing with Shapes

Keywords: shape 形状 (xíngzhuàng), circle 圆形 (yuánxíng), square 正方形 (zhèngfāngxíng), rectangle 长方形 (chángfāngxíng), triangle 三角形 (sānjiǎoxíng)
Activity 1 (15 min): "Shape Robot" — Students create a robot using only geometric shapes. Each shape must be a different color.

Activity 2 (15 min): "Shape Scavenger Hunt" — Students walk around the classroom finding real objects that match shapes, then draw them in Tux Paint.

Activity 3 (10 min): Shape counting — Students count and label how many of each shape they used.
Formative: Used at least 4 different shapes with labels.

Week 15 — Colors and Fill Tools

Keywords: fill tool 填充工具 (tiánchōng gōngjù), gradient 渐变 (jiànbiàn), primary colors 原色 (yuánsè), secondary colors 间色 (jiánsè), shade 阴影 (yīnyǐng)
Activity 1 (15 min): "Rainbow Landscape" — Students draw a landscape with a rainbow, green grass, blue sky, yellow sun, brown tree. Focus on using the fill bucket tool.

Activity 2 (15 min): "Color Mixing" — Students create a color wheel showing primary and secondary colors in the paint program.

Activity 3 (10 min): Color vocabulary game — Teacher says a color in English; students find and use it within 5 seconds.
Formative: Accurate use of fill tool; picture contains 5+ colors.

Week 16 — Introduction to Word Processing

Keywords: word processor 文字处理器 (wénzì chǔlǐqì), document 文档 (wéndàng), text 文本 (wénběn), font 字体 (zìtǐ), cursor 光标 (guāngbiāo)
Activity 1 (15 min): "Open Word" — Teacher models opening Microsoft Word / Google Docs. Students follow along.

Activity 2 (15 min): "Type My Favorite Animal" — Students type the sentence: "My favorite animal is a ______ because ______." Focus on spaces and periods.

Activity 3 (10 min): Saving practice — Students save their document with their name as the filename.
Formative: Document saved correctly; sentence contains correct spaces and punctuation.

Week 17 — Formatting Text

Keywords: bold 粗体 (cūtǐ), italic 斜体 (xiétǐ), underline 下划线 (xiàhuàxiàn), font size 字号 (zìhào), font color 字体颜色 (zìtǐ yánsè)
Activity 1 (15 min): "Make it BIG, Make it Small!" — Students type the word "HELLO" and format it in 5 different sizes, from 8pt to 72pt.

Activity 2 (15 min): "Crazy Text" — Students type their name and apply bold, italic, underline, and a color of their choice.

Activity 3 (10 min): "Format Detective" — Teacher shows a formatted text; students recreate it exactly.
Formative: Can apply bold, italic, and change font size independently.

Week 18 — Adding Pictures to Documents

Keywords: insert 插入 (chārù), image 图片 (túpiàn), resize 调整大小 (tiáozhěng dàxiǎo), move 移动 (yídòng), caption 标题 (biāotí)
Activity 1 (15 min): "Picture + Caption" — Students insert a picture of an animal and type a sentence underneath: "This is a ______. It is ______."

Activity 2 (15 min): "Resize and Move" — Students practice making a picture bigger, smaller, and moving it to different positions on the page.

Activity 3 (10 min): "All About Me" page — Students insert their own photo (pre-taken by teacher) and write 3 sentences about themselves.
Formative: Picture inserted and resized; caption matches picture.

Week 19 — Digital Storytelling

Keywords: story 故事 (gùshi), character 角色 (juésè), setting 场景 (chǎngjǐng), beginning 开始 (kāishǐ), ending 结尾 (jiéwěi)
Activity 1 (15 min): "Story Elements Song" — Students learn a song: "Every story has a beginning, middle, and end!"

Activity 2 (15 min): "My Weekend Story" — Students type 3–5 sentences about their weekend: "On Saturday I... On Sunday I..."

Activity 3 (10 min): Illustration — Students draw a picture in Tux Paint to match their story and insert it into their document.
Formative: Story has a beginning, middle, and end; includes picture.

Week 20 — Creating a Simple Book

Activity 1 (15 min): "Book Template" — Students receive a 3-page template: Page 1 = Title + Author; Page 2 = Story; Page 3 = "The End" + picture.

Activity 2 (15 min): Writing time — Students write their book using the template, with bilingual word bank support.

Activity 3 (10 min): Peer sharing — Students swap computers and read each other's books.
Formative: Book has 3 pages, title, text, and picture.

Week 21 — Adding Speech Bubbles

Keywords: speech bubble 对话框 (duìhuà kuàng), dialogue 对话 (duìhuà), comic strip 连环画 (liánhuánhuà)
Activity 1 (15 min): "Comic Strip Creation" — Students create a 3-panel comic strip showing a conversation between two animals or characters.

Activity 2 (15 min): Students add speech bubbles with simple dialogue: "Hello!" "How are you?" "I am happy!"

Activity 3 (10 min): Gallery walk — Students walk around and read 3 classmates' comics.
Formative: Comic strip has 3 panels with dialogue in speech bubbles.

Week 22 — Review & Share

Activity 1 (20 min): "Digital Portfolio Setup" — Students organize their work into a folder: "My Digital Art," "My Stories," "My Comics."

Activity 2 (15 min): Story sharing circle — Volunteers read their story aloud while displaying it on the projector.

Activity 3 (5 min): Self-assessment — Students color a smiley face: 😊 I did great! 🙂 I tried. 😐 I need help.

Week 23 — Multimedia Creation

Keywords: multimedia 多媒体 (duōméitǐ), recording 录音 (lùyīn), microphone 麦克风 (màikèfēng), play button 播放按钮 (bōfàng ànniǔ)
Activity 1 (15 min): "Record My Voice" — Students record themselves reading their story aloud using a simple voice recorder or PowerPoint narration.

Activity 2 (15 min): "Voice + Picture" — Students match their recording to their story page.

Activity 3 (10 min): Listening party — Small groups listen to each other's recordings.
Formative: Recording completed and matched to story.

Week 24 — Term 2 Assessment

Project Assessment (25 min): Students create a 3-page digital story with:
  • Title page with formatted title (bold, colored)
  • Story page with at least 3 sentences and one inserted picture
  • "The End" page with a self-drawn picture
Peer Review (10 min): Partner reads the story and gives a compliment using sentence frame: "I like your ______ because ______."

Teacher Review (5 min): Checklist assessment using Bronze/Silver/Gold criteria.
