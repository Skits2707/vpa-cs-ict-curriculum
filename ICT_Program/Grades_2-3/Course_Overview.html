VPA ICT Program — Grades 2-3: Digital Foundations

Academic Year: 2024–2025 | Total Weeks: 36 | Lesson Duration: 40 minutes

Target Group: EAL learners (Mandarin L1), mixed ability | Standards: CSTA K-12, Common Core ELA/Math

Program Vision

Our Grades 2–3 ICT curriculum introduces young learners to the digital world through play-based, hands-on experiences. Students develop foundational skills in typing, digital creation, online safety, and computational thinking while building English vocabulary through bilingual scaffolding.

Assessment Overview

Formative (ongoing):

  • Observation checklists during activities
  • Exit tickets with bilingual vocabulary
  • Peer sharing and feedback

Summative (term-end):

  • Practical skills demonstration
  • Digital portfolio (collection of work)
  • Teacher-guided interview assessment

Bilingual Support Strategy

Every lesson includes bilingual keywords (中文 / Pinyin) displayed on the board. Visual icons accompany all instructions. Sentence frames support EAL learners:

  • "I can..." / "我会... (wǒ huì...)"
  • "First, I... Then, I..." / "首先... 然后... (shǒuxiān... ránhòu...)"
  • "This is my..." / "这是我的... (zhè shì wǒ de...)"
