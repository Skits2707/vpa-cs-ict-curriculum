Usage Guide 使用指南

English: This glossary is organised by topic and marked with age-appropriate tags. Teachers should introduce Simple terms first (Grades 2–5), then progress to All Ages and finally Technical terms (Grades 6–IGCSE). Pinyin is provided to support pronunciation for Chinese language learners.

中文：本词汇表按主题组织，并标注了适合不同年龄的标签。教师应先教授简单词汇（2-5年级），然后过渡到全年龄段，最后教授技术性词汇（6年级至IGCSE）。提供拼音以支持中文学习者的发音练习。

Grade Bands 年级分组:
• Grades 2–3: Focus on Digital Basics simple terms + Productivity simple terms
• Grades 4–5: Add Internet & Safety + Multimedia simple terms
• Grades 6–8: Introduce Programming, Databases, Hardware, Networks technical terms
• Grades 9–10 (IGCSE): Full glossary mastery required for examination success
