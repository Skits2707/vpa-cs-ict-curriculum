A comprehensive guide to careers that build on ICT skills, showing how VPA's curriculum prepares students for each pathway. Includes required skills, qualifications, salary ranges, and VPA preparation steps.

Career Pathways Overview

English: ICT skills open doors to diverse career paths across four main categories: Digital Literacy careers (using technology effectively), Technical careers (building technology), Creative careers (designing with technology), and Entrepreneurial careers (leading with technology). Each pathway builds on foundations laid in the VPA ICT curriculum.

中文：ICT技能为四大类职业道路打开大门：数字素养职业（有效使用技术）、技术职业（构建技术）、创意职业（用技术设计）和创业职业（用技术领导）。每条道路都建立在VPA ICT课程奠定的基础之上。

VPA ICT → Career Readiness Checklist

By End of...Students Should Be Able To...Career Readiness
Grade 5 Touch type at 30 WPM; create formatted documents and presentations; search safely; explain online safety rules Ready for digital literacy roles with supervision
Grade 8 Build a simple website; write Python programs with loops and conditionals; design a database; edit images and video Ready for creative and technical internships; prepared for IGCSE
Grade 10 (IGCSE) Produce examination-standard documents; build data models; write SQL; create multi-page websites; understand networks and security Ready for entry-level technical roles; fully prepared for IB DP CS
Grade 12 (IB DP) Design and implement OOP solutions; analyse algorithms; understand systems architecture; complete a 2000-word CS investigation Ready for university CS programmes; competitive for internships at tech companies

Further Education Pathways

UK: University degrees in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Digital Media, or Business Information Systems. Apprenticeships (Degree Apprenticeships) combine work and study. Notable: Imperial College London, University of Cambridge, University of Oxford, University of Manchester.
US: Bachelor's in CS, Information Science, or HCI. Strong emphasis on portfolio and internships. Notable: MIT, Stanford, Carnegie Mellon, UC Berkeley, Georgia Tech.
China: Bachelor's in 计算机科学与技术 (Computer Science), 软件工程 (Software Engineering), or 数字媒体技术 (Digital Media Technology). Notable: Tsinghua University, Peking University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Zhejiang University.
Hong Kong: Bachelor's in CS, Information Engineering, or Multimedia Computing. Notable: University of Hong Kong (HKU), Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK).

深造途径：英国——计算机科学、软件工程、数字媒体或商业信息系统学位；美国——CS、信息科学或HCI学士学位，重视作品集和实习；中国——计算机科学与技术、软件工程或数字媒体技术学士学位；香港——计算机科学、信息工程或多媒体计算学士学位。
