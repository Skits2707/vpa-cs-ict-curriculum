Paper 2 Marking Principles

Evidence-based marking: Examiners mark BOTH the printed evidence AND the electronic files. Students must:

  • ☐ Save all work to the designated folder with exact filenames specified
  • ☐ Print evidence as requested (usually the final document)
  • ☐ Not include their name in filenames unless instructed
  • ☐ Not use templates or pre-prepared files
