Practical Assessment Format

Paper 2 (2 hours 30 minutes, 80 marks):

  • Students receive an electronic source file and an examination paper with instructions
  • All work must be completed on computer and saved to designated folder
  • Examiner marks printed evidence + electronic files
  • Students need to demonstrate BOTH technical skills AND accuracy

Semester Objectives

By the end of Semester 2, students will be able to:

  • ☐ Create and format complex documents with styles, tables, and mail merge
  • ☐ Build spreadsheets with formulas, functions, charts, and conditional formatting
  • ☐ Design professional presentations with master slides, transitions, and embedded media
  • ☐ Work accurately under timed conditions
  • ☐ Follow complex instructions precisely
  • ☐ Proofread and self-correct their work
