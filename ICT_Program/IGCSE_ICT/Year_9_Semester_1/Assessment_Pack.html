Mark Scheme Guidance for Teachers

IGCSE uses three types of marks:

  • A marks: Accuracy — correct answer
  • M marks: Method — correct method even if calculation error
  • B marks: Independent — standalone marks for correct statements

Paper 1 Command Words

CommandWhat Students Must DoExample
StateGive a brief answer, no explanation needed"State two input devices"
DescribeGive a detailed account with features"Describe how a laser printer works"
ExplainGive reasons or causes, show how/why"Explain why a SSD is faster than a HDD"
CompareIdentify similarities AND differences"Compare star and bus topologies"
DiscussGive balanced arguments for and against"Discuss the effects of teleworking"
SuggestApply knowledge to a new scenario"Suggest a suitable device for a warehouse"

Sample Mark Scheme: 6-Mark "Discuss" Question

Question: "Discuss the effects of increased ICT use on employment." [6 marks]

Mark Scheme (B marks):

  • Job creation in tech sectors (1 mark)
  • Job displacement through automation (1 mark)
  • Need for retraining/reskilling (1 mark)
  • New types of jobs (e.g., data analyst, UX designer) (1 mark)
  • Reduction in manual/repetitive jobs (1 mark)
  • Global outsourcing opportunities (1 mark)
  • Balanced conclusion with justification (1 mark — any 6 points)

Bilingual Assessment Support

  • Key terms tests: English term → Chinese definition, and vice versa
  • Sentence frames for "explain":
    • "This is because..." / "这是因为... (zhè shì yīnwèi...)"
    • "One reason is... Another reason is..." / "一个原因是...另一个原因是..."
    • "For example,..." / "例如,... (lìrú,...)"
  • Past paper annotations: Chinese summaries of question requirements
  • Visual mark schemes: Diagram-based marking for hardware questions
