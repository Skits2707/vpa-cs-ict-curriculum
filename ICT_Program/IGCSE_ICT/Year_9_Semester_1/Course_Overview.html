Assessment Structure

Paper 1 Theory (2 hours, 100 marks):

  • Section A: Short-answer questions (approx. 30 marks)
  • Section B: Structured questions requiring longer responses (approx. 70 marks)

Question Types:

  • Knowledge recall (define, state, list)
  • Understanding (explain, describe, compare)
  • Application (suggest, discuss, evaluate)

Semester Learning Objectives

By the end of Semester 1, students will be able to:

  • ☐ Describe the components of computer systems and their functions
  • ☐ Compare input and output devices for different scenarios
  • ☐ Evaluate storage solutions based on capacity, speed, portability, and durability
  • ☐ Explain network types, topologies, and protocols
  • ☐ Identify and use appropriate data types in ICT contexts
  • ☐ Analyze the social, economic, and environmental effects of ICT
  • ☐ Describe threats to data security and appropriate countermeasures
  • ☐ Outline the systems development life cycle
