📖 VPA IGCSE ICT Year 10 Semester 2 — Course Overview

Victoria Park Academy | IGCSE ICT (0417) | Year 10 Semester 2
Duration: 18 weeks | Focus: Consolidation of all IGCSE topics + Mock Examination Preparation
This semester bridges the gap between topic completion and examination readiness, ensuring students are confident across all three papers.

Student Profile

Target Cohort: Year 10 students (ages 14–15) preparing for IGCSE ICT examination in Year 11.

Entry Profile: Students have completed Year 10 Semester 1, covering:

  • Document production (word processing, formatting, mail merge)
  • Data manipulation (spreadsheets, formulas, charts, pivot tables)
  • Presentations (slide design, transitions, multimedia)
  • Data analysis (modelling, what-if analysis, goal seek)
  • Web authoring (HTML, CSS, multi-page sites)
  • Databases (design, relationships, queries, forms, reports)
  • Networks and security (protocols, encryption, threats)

Exit Profile: By the end of this semester, students will:

  • Demonstrate fluency across all IGCSE ICT practical skills
  • Complete timed examination-style tasks under mock conditions
  • Identify personal areas of strength and weakness through gap analysis
  • Develop effective examination technique and time management
  • Be prepared to enter Year 11 with a clear revision and improvement plan

学生概况：本课程面向10年级学生（14-15岁），为11年级IGCSE ICT考试做准备。学生已完成10年级第一学期，涵盖文档制作、数据处理、演示文稿、数据分析、网页制作、数据库和网络与安全。本学期结束时，学生将熟练掌握所有IGCSE ICT实践技能，完成模拟考试条件下的计时任务，通过差距分析识别个人优势和弱点，发展有效的考试技巧和时间管理，并为进入11年级制定清晰的复习和改进计划。

EAL Checklist

English as an Additional Language Support:

  • ☐ Bilingual glossary provided for all technical terms (EN/中文)
  • ☐ Examination instructions read aloud in addition to written form
  • ☐ Extra time (25%) available for timed assessments upon application
  • ☐ Word banks provided for extended-response questions
  • ☐ Visual aids and diagrams encouraged in written responses
  • ☐ Peer translation support during consolidation (not during mocks)
  • ☐ Simplified English versions of case studies and scenarios
  • ☐ One-to-one check-ins with EAL coordinator every 3 weeks

EAL支持清单：为所有技术术语提供双语词汇表（英/中）；口头朗读考试说明；可申请额外25%时间；为扩展回答题提供词汇库；鼓励书面回答中使用视觉辅助和图表；巩固阶段提供同伴翻译支持（模拟考试期间不提供）；提供案例研究和场景的简化英语版本；每3周与EAL协调员进行一对一检查。

Differentiation

Bronze Level — Developing

SupportDescription
Scaffolded Tasks Step-by-step worksheets with partially completed examples for each practical skill
Extended Time Additional 15 minutes for consolidation tasks; 25% extra for assessments
Peer Support Pairing with Silver/Gold students for guided practice; structured peer tutoring
Simplified Instructions Chunked instructions with visual cues; numbered steps with checkboxes
Focus Areas Prioritise core skills: basic document formatting, simple formulas, basic HTML/CSS, simple queries

Silver Level — Secure

SupportDescription
Standard Tasks Full examination-style tasks with standard time limits
Independent Practice Self-directed consolidation with teacher check-ins
Extension Opportunities Optional challenge tasks: macros, advanced CSS, complex SQL with JOINs
Peer Teaching Opportunities to explain concepts to Bronze students (reinforces learning)
Focus Areas Develop fluency across all topics; refine examination technique; build speed

Gold Level — Advanced

SupportDescription
Stretch Tasks Advanced challenges: VBA scripting, JavaScript interactivity, database normalisation to 3NF, responsive design
Leadership Roles Peer tutor for Bronze/Silver students; lead study groups; create revision resources
Competition Preparation Prepare for Bebras, UKMT, or coding competitions alongside IGCSE preparation
Research Projects Independent investigation into emerging ICT topics (AI, quantum computing, blockchain)
Focus Areas Achieve examination excellence; develop transferable skills for IB DP Computer Science
