Subject Combination Advice

Ideal IB DP Subject Combination for CS:
  • HL: Mathematics: Analysis and Approaches (essential), Computer Science (strongly recommended), Physics or Chemistry (recommended for some programmes)
  • SL: English A Language and Literature, a second language, a humanities subject (Economics, Psychology, or Philosophy)
  • EE: Computer Science Extended Essay is highly regarded by admissions tutors

Alternative for students not taking HL CS: HL Maths AA + HL Physics + one other HL science is acceptable for most programmes, but HL CS is advantageous for CS-specific courses.

Scholarship Application Tips

  • Start early: Many scholarship deadlines are earlier than admission deadlines (e.g., Gates Cambridge: October; Rhodes: October)
  • Track record matters: Scholarship committees look for sustained impact, not just grades — show leadership, community service, and initiative
  • Essays are critical: Most scholarships require additional essays — start drafting in G11 summer
  • Recommendations: Ask teachers who know you well and can speak to your character, not just academic ability
  • Financial documentation: Need-based scholarships require detailed family financial information — prepare early

奖学金申请建议：尽早开始——许多奖学金截止日期早于录取截止日期；成绩记录很重要——奖学金委员会看重持续影响力，而不仅仅是成绩；论文至关重要——大多数奖学金需要额外论文，G11暑假开始起草；推荐信——请了解你并能评价你品格的老师撰写；财务文件——需要奖学金要求详细的家庭财务信息——提前准备。

Gap Year & Deferral Options

  • UK: Most universities allow deferral for 1 year; gap year activities should be meaningful (internships, research, travel, volunteering)
  • US: Deferral policies vary; some universities encourage gap years (Harvard, MIT, Yale)
  • China: Deferral is generally not permitted; students must apply in the year they intend to enrol
  • Hong Kong: Most universities allow 1-year deferral with valid reason
