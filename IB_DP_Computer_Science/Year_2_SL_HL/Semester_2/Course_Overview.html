Semester Overview

The final semester is focused on completing all remaining syllabus content, finalising the Internal Assessment, and intensive examination preparation. HL students study Topic 7 (Control) and prepare for Paper 3 (Case Study). All students sit mock examinations and receive targeted intervention based on gap analysis.

Final Revision Strategy

  • Retrieval practice: Daily low-stakes quizzes on rotating topics
  • Interleaving: Mixed-topic questions to strengthen discrimination
  • Elaboration: Students explain concepts to peers in own words
  • Concrete examples: Every abstract concept linked to real-world example
  • Dual coding: Diagrams + words combined in all revision materials
