Revision and Final Examinations 复习与期末考试 Fùxí yǔ Qímò Kǎoshì

Level: SL + HL | Duration: 9 weeks | Goal: Examination readiness

Overview

The final 9 weeks are dedicated to intensive revision, mock examinations, gap analysis, and targeted intervention. All students should finish this period confident in their subject knowledge, examination technique, and IA quality.

Weeks 17–18 — Examination Period

During the official IB examination period:

  • No new content is taught
  • Optional drop-in revision sessions offered daily
  • Individual tutorials available on request
  • Past paper "warm-up" sessions before each exam
  • Well-being check-ins; stress management support

Well-being During Revision

Student Well-being Checklist:
  • ☐ Sleep: aim for 8 hours per night
  • ☐ Breaks: 5-minute break every 25 minutes (Pomodoro technique)
  • ☐ Exercise: at least 30 minutes daily
  • ☐ Nutrition: regular meals; hydrate
  • ☐ Social connection: talk to friends and family
  • ☐ Help-seeking: ask teachers early if struggling
