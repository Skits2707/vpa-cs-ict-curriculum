Target: Standard Level (SL) Students | Duration: 18 weeks parallel with HL topics

Programme Structure

While HL students study Topics 5 and 6, SL students follow a structured revision programme covering all four SL topics (1–4) while simultaneously developing their Internal Assessment. This ensures SL students are exam-ready while completing their IA to the highest standard.

Weeks 10–12: Intensive IA Development

Lessons split between supervised IA development and targeted revision:

  • Monday/Tuesday: Supervised IA coding time with teacher circulation
  • Wednesday: IA troubleshooting clinic (common errors, debugging support)
  • Thursday: Topic rotation revision (Topics 1–4 cycle)
  • Friday: Retrieval quiz + IA progress check
