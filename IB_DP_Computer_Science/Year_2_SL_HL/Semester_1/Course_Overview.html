TOK This Semester

Topic 5: Abstract data structures like trees and graphs are mathematical abstractions. Do they exist independently of their computer implementations? Is a "tree" in code the same kind of thing as a tree in nature—or is it merely a useful fiction?

Topic 6: Operating systems allocate resources "fairly" using algorithms. What does "fair" mean in this context? Is fairness a mathematical property, a social value, or both?
